Simone Falcomer is a photographer and Art Director based in Barcelona.
Born in Italy, he studied photojournalism at Grisart Academy in Barcelona, where he developed a solid and personal approach to the image.
He is currently studying Fine Arts in Barcelona while working as a freelance photographer across fashion, documentary and artistic practice.
His work is in constant evolution — each project brings him into unknown territory, always outside his comfort zone.
He shoots on analogue film, working in 35mm and medium format. He uses photography as a tool, believing his fullest expression is still ahead of him.
Through the lens, Simone chases time as it passes. He reflects on the relativity of time and on the inner and outer transformations of himself, of people, and of the world.
His visual language draws from mid-20th century photography, neoclassical sculpture, Baroque painting and Italian Renaissance art.
Editorial work published in Vogue Nederland, Neo2, Kultblut Magazine, Vein Magazine and Cake Magazine. Commercial clients include Marni and Outsiders Division. Image Director at Acceptance Letter Studio.
