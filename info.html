Simone Falcomer
Photographer & Art Director — Barcelona

Simone Falcomer is a photographer and Art Director based in Barcelona. Born in Italy, he studied photojournalism at Grisart Academy in Barcelona, where he developed a solid and personal approach to the image. He is currently studying Fine Arts in Barcelona while working as a freelance photographer across fashion, documentary and artistic practice.

His work is in constant evolution — each project brings him into unknown territory, always outside his comfort zone. He shoots on analogue film, working in 35mm and medium format, and manages digital studio production. He uses photography as a tool, believing his fullest expression is still ahead of him — a search for ways to capture what he feels.

Through the lens, Simone chases time as it passes. He spends hours reflecting on the relativity of time and on the inner and outer transformations of himself, of people, and of the world. His visual language draws from mid-20th century photography, neoclassical sculpture, Baroque painting and Italian Renaissance art. Although he plans his work methodically, he always moves toward surprising and unconventional results.

His editorial work has been published in Vogue Nederland, Neo2, Kultblut Magazine, Vein Magazine and Cake Magazine. His commercial clients include Marni and Outsiders Division. He works internally as Image Director at Acceptance Letter Studio.
